WHO WE ARE

Team Reaper #22360 is a fourth-year FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics team based at Forest Hills High School in Queens, New York. Founded with the goal of creating STEM opportunities for underrepresented and disadvantaged youth in 2022, our team of dedicated juniors and seniors is passionate about innovation, collaboration,and community impact. We compete annually in the FIRST Tech Challenge, where we design, build, and program robots. We have advanced to the NYC Championships for the first time in our history, earning the 2nd Place Inspire Award, and winning 1st Place in Design. Beyond competitions, we mentor students at J.H.S. 157 Halsey, Russell Sage, and P.S. 220, guiding them in programming, CAD, and engineering. We’ve also hosted hands-on robotics workshops at the Queens Public Library and collaborated with Paramount Global to organize large-scale STEM events that have reached over a thousand students.
