Thank You,

Paramount Global!

We extend our deepest gratitude to Paramount Global for their generous support and collaboration with Reapers Robotics FTC #22360. Paramount Global helped us host the successful THRIVE IN STEM event for underrepresented youth groups. This initiative hosted over 70+ attendees and featured vendors from companies like Google, Adobe, and TKS.This partnership was a huge step in achieving our mission to create STEM opportunities and exposure for students who may not otherwise have access to them.
