Team Reapers #22360

Hello! We are The Reapers, a student-led robotics team currently participating in First Tech Challenge (FTC). We are a part of Forest Hills High School. Our team consists of 19 members this year with Juniors and Seniors. We all share the roles of programming, engineering, and outreach.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share the values of FIRST and expand access to STEM education, especially for underrepresented students who may not otherwise have these opportunities. We believe robotics is more than competition; it is a way to inspire innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving in the next generation.
